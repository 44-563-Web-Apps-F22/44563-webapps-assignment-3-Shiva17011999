Shiva Kumar Allam

Kakatiya Museum

Its single-story building contains a collection of Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain sculpture. Along with the Marble Buddha and Chamunda, the garden also contains a sizable Nandi and other Saivite statues. Additionally, it has Shanmukha or Skanda from the 12th-century Veerabhadra image as well as the Parsvanatha image from the 11th century. Additionally, it contains a collection of local ceramic shards and coins from antiquity.
